About Us

Introducing RevealPrints: Custom Scratch Off Poster for Children's Education Are you searching for an innovative and interactive way to enhance children's education? Look no further than RevealPrints! Our Custom Scratch Off Poster is a perfect tool to engage, educate, and inspire young minds. With RevealPrints, children will embark on an exciting journey of learning and discovery. Each area of our poster is covered by a scratch-off layer, revealing fun and educational activities underneath. As they scratch off each area, kids will eagerly explore a variety of school and teaching activities specially designed to promote learning in a playful manner. Our activities cover a wide range of subjects, ensuring a well-rounded educational experience. From math challenges and science experiments to creative writing prompts and art projects, there's something for every child's interest and ability. As they complete these activities, children will develop critical thinking skills, boost their creativity, and reinforce what they've learned in the classroom. But the excitement doesn't stop there! Once children have scratched off every area of the poster, a stunning display of custom, school-appropriate artwork is revealed. This artwork has been carefully curated by local artists to support mental health charities. By incorporating these meaningful designs, RevealPrints aims to not only educate children but also promote awareness and empathy towards mental health issues. RevealPrints is more than just another educational tool; it's a transformative experience. It allows children to actively participate in their education, fostering a love for learning while supporting a worthy cause. Parents, teachers, and educators can rest assured that RevealPrints' Custom Scratch Off Poster will engage and inspire children, making learning a fun and memorable adventure. Join us in revolutionizing children's education with RevealPrints. Made with passion and care, our veteran-owned company brings joy and knowledge into the lives of young learners, one scratch at a time.
